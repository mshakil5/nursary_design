Our Age Groups

Child-centred care for every stage

Welcome to Angelina’s Day Care – Toddler Room (3 to 12 Months)

Our Toddler Room offers a warm, caring space tailored for babies aged 3 to 12 months. We support each child’s rapid development with gentle care and meaningful play.

Nurturing Early Development

With close and trusting relationships, our experienced team supports your baby’s wellbeing and growth in key areas:

  • Physical movement
  • Language and communication
  • Social-emotional bonding
  • Creative play
Learning Through Exploration

Your child will enjoy soft toys, music, sensory play and nature-based materials—all designed to spark curiosity and early learning.

A Calm Transition

We partner with families to create a smooth, happy start to nursery life, respecting your child’s routines and needs.

Welcome to Angelina’s Day Care – Two to Three’s Room

In this room, we support your child’s growing independence. With gentle guidance, they’ll explore toilet training, self-feeding and dressing themselves. Please pack extra clothes—just in case!

Learning Through Play

Children enjoy:

  • Role play in our home corner
  • Building with big blocks
  • Creative sand and water play
  • A cozy reading nook
  • Outdoor fun for movement and balance

Your child will gain confidence, curiosity, and key self-care skills—all through play!

Welcome to Angelina’s Day Care – Preschool Room

Our Preschool Room nurtures your child’s growing confidence and school readiness. Children practice independence through activities like dressing, toileting, and making simple choices during the day.

Learning Through Play

Preschoolers enjoy:

  • Imaginative role play and storytelling
  • Early writing and number games
  • Creative arts and crafts
  • Science and nature exploration
  • Active outdoor play to build coordination and teamwork

We help your child grow into a curious, capable, and confident learner—ready for the next big step!
