Toddler Room

Angelina’s Day Care is particularly designed for babies from six months to one year old, working on a maximum of 3:1 child-to-staff ratio. It provides a child’s rapid development in a safe environment. Also, facilitates each personnel to provide deep care of the child’s growth, viewing them as an individual.

Activities

Moreover, your baby’s growth is directed on several prime areas, which are:

  • Rapid development of your child.
  • Language & communication.
  • Individual, group, and expressive development.
  • Our aim is to frame such key areas through fun activities, talking, and play.
  • Use of toys, natural light, adult interaction, and sensory experiences to explore the world.
  • Ensuring a seamless transition from home to nursery life.
