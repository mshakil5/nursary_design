FEES & TERM TIMES

We are open as usual Mon – Fri: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Term Times

We are open Monday to Friday between 8:00 AM and 6.00 PM and 51 weeks of the year excluding Bank Holidays. The nursery is closed between Christmas and New Year.

Fees information

There is a non-refundable registration fee of £50.
A deposit of £100 needs to be payable upon registration, which will be deducted from the last month’s nursery fees. For full fee information, please contact us.

Discounts

Opening discount of full-time places receive a 2-week discount on nursery fees. Therefore, the total payment will be 49 weeks instead of 51 weeks. Sibling discount of 10%. Discount for Met Police, NHS staff, Fire Brigade staff, and Teachers of 10%.

Funding

15 hours Free Childcare for 2 Year Old’s

  • In the UK for working families, including independent contractors.
  • Having a weekly income of at least £142 (equivalent to 16 hours at the National Minimum or Living Wage) and less than £100k each
  • Who do not receive childcare vouchers, Universal Credit, or Tax Credits?
  • With kids 0–11 (or 0–16 if they’re disabled)
  • The government will add £2 to every £8 you deposit into an online account, up to a maximum of £2,000 per kid annually.
  • Please understand how the modifications may influence your eligibility before applying or reconfirming if the coronavirus (COVID-19) has changed your working pattern or income.

15 Hours of Free Daycare for Children Ages 3 and 4 Years old

  • For all families in England
  • With kids ages three and four
  • For 38 weeks, 15 hours of free nursery or early education
  • A total of 570 hours annually that you may use as needed with one or more daycare centres
  • You can “stretch” the hours over 52 weeks with some providers by using fewer hours each week.

30 Hours of Free Childcare for 3 & 4-Year-Olds

  • For working-class households in England
  • With kids ages three and four
  • For 38 weeks, 30 hours of free nursery or early education
  • 1,140 hours total annually that you can utilise however you see fit with one or more daycare providers
  • You can “stretch” the hours over 52 weeks with some providers by using fewer hours each week.
  • Before you apply or reconfirm for 30 hours, please read how changes to your working pattern and/or income due to the coronavirus (COVID-19) may influence your eligibility.

For additional details, please go to childcarechoices.gov.uk
