Meal Times at Angelina's Day Care

Meal Times at Angelina's Day Care

At Angelina's Day Care, a fresh food nursery in Colchester, we take pride in offering wholesome, nutritious meals throughout the day across all of our programmes. Meals are planned to support your child's growth and wellbeing.

Daily Meal Schedule
  • 8:00 AM for Breakfast
  • 10:00 AM for Mid-morning Snack
  • 12:00 PM for Cooked Lunch
  • 2:30 PM for Afternoon Snack
  • 4:00 PM for Tea
Our Nursery Food philosophy

We carefully plan each meal to provide essential nutrients that support your child's development. Our team uses quality fresh ingredients and creates a varied seasonal menu.

Sample Daily Menu

Example menu for a regular day at the nursery:

  • Breakfast: Wholegrain cereal and milk
  • Mid-morning snack: Crackers or fresh fruit
  • Lunch: Chicken or veggie dishes with sides
  • Afternoon snack: Bread, fruit, milk
  • Tea: Sandwiches or light meal
